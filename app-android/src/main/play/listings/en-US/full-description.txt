This application adds Grand River Transit (GRT) ION rapid transit system information to MonTransit.

This app contains the light-rail schedule (available offline) and the real-time next departures from realtimemap.grt.ca as well as the latest news from www.grt.ca and @GRT_ROW & @rideIONrt on Twitter.

GRT light rail serves Region of Waterloo in Ontario, Canada.

Once this application is installed, the MonTransit app will display light rail information (schedule...).

This application only has a temporary icon: download the MonTransit app (free) in the "More ..." section bellow or by following this Google Play link https://goo.gl/pCk5mV

You can install this application on the SD card but it is not recommended.

The information comes from the GTFS file provided by the Region of Waterloo and Grand River Transit.
https://www.grt.ca/en/about-grt/open-data.aspx

This application is free and open-source:
https://github.com/mtransitapps/ca-grand-river-transit-light-rail-android

This app is not related with the Region of Waterloo and Grand River Transit.

Permissions:
- Other: required to load real-time schedule and read news from www.grt.ca and Twitter
